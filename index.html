About Us

This dashboard presents a data-driven exploration of housing market trends by analyzing sale prices alongside key property features such as location, renovation status, house age, and living area size. Through interactive visualizations, users can uncover patterns, compare market segments, and understand how different attributes influence property value. The analysis helps transform raw housing data into meaningful insights, enabling better decision-making for buyers, sellers, and analysts by revealing hidden relationships and emerging trends within the real estate market.

The housing market is influenced by multiple factors, and visual analytics makes it easier to understand these complex relationships. This visualization highlights how variables such as property size, renovation status, age of the house, and neighborhood characteristics impact sale prices over time. By presenting the data through interactive charts and comparative views, the dashboard allows users to identify trends, spot pricing patterns, and gain clearer insights into market behavior. The goal is to provide an intuitive and engaging way to explore housing data and support informed real estate decisions.
